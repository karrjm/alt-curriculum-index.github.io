ABOUT

The Critical Technology Curriculum Index is a personal tool for gathering educational resources that teach technology while simultaneously critiquing it. This project is inspired by Mindy Seu's Cyberfeminism Index, which documents the past and present of cyberfeminism.

This index aims to support educators, students, and practitioners interested in developing a critical relationship with technology, challenging techno-solutionism, and exploring alternative approaches to computing education.
