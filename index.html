INDEX OF CYBERFEMINIST PEDAGOGY

This is an index of alternative curricula, syllabi, lesson plans, and workshops that approach the teaching of technology from a Cyberfeminist position.
“The simplest one-liner: it's a critical use of technology. In a more complex version, I might refer back to the cybernetic origins of this prefix 'cyber.' Thinking about feedback loops, how we disseminate feminism through online channels. How we are critical of technology through the use of technology."
— Mindy Seu, when asked of her definition of Cyberfeminism.

Much like Mindy Seu's Cyberfeminism Index, which documents the past and present of cyberfeminism, this project aims to serve as a resource for educators interested in developing a critical relationship with technology.
